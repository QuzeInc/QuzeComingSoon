TO PUT EDUCATION ON BLOCKCHAIN, WORLDWIDE.

A vault for all your accomplishments and accolades. We aim to create a world where every individual has a way to learn what he really wants to learn. With an amazing foundational technology like blockchain and other futuretech like Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing and many more, we have the power to create an entirely new substructure for existing education system. Today the authorities are not aware about the utility and benefits of these technologies. We envision a future where acquiring and monetizing the skills would be on click of a button. Our platform would bridge the gap between online education and traditional classroom learning by providing a blockchain based solutions that allows to gain personalized learning paths and acquire new skills.



Learners

Learners

People who are interested to learn new skills.The student public vault would be having the record of all the skills he acquired. The student has complete ownership of his records but can't falsify them



Educators

Educators

People who wish to provide the skills and monetize their skills. This platform creates a distributed yet structural system of learning to provide people means of transferring skills outside school hours.



Employers

Employers

Organizations that seek to hire the right kind of candidate for a job can look for them on our platform with blockchain verified skills. The employers will be able to verify all through access to his vault



Institutions

Institutions

They will act as the quality checkers for the content. They will provide and verify the content and ensuring the quality of the courses. Any institution would be able to push in records onto the students profile.
